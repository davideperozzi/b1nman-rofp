_HOWTO

The goal is simple. Stay alive!

Down below you see a "power bar". This bar shows you the remaining power of B1NMAN.

You lose power by the following actions: Shooting, getting hit.
You receive power by killing enemies.

You play against two enemies. The time and the robots.
Each wave lasts 30 seconds.

The game is over if the time passed or your power bar is empty.

_CONTROLS

[SPACE]

Shoot (Keep pressed)

[ARROW_LEFT]

Walk left

[ARROW_RIGHT]

Walk right

[ARROW_UP]

Jump

_ENEMIES

Emits an impulse after a time.
If you're intersecting with this impulse you lose some power.
This enemy can be move for a tactical advantage.

Moves if you're moving or shooting.
If you intersect with him you lose some power and he destroys himself.
You can't say what this guy is gonna do next.

_HINTS

Kill with style! (It saves you power)
